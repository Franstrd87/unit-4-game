Calories Collector

  1. You will be given a random number of calories at the start of the game.
  2. There are four junk foods below. By clicking on a food you will add a specific amount of calories to your total calories.
  3. The player wins if the total number of calories equals the number of calories needed to win.
  4. The value of each food is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each food.
